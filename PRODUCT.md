# Product Context

## Users
- Book lovers and home library enthusiasts
- Day-to-day users who want to catalog their personal book collections
- Non-technical users who want a simple, delightful experience

## Product Purpose
A bookshelf scanning app that uses AI to detect and identify books from photos of bookshelves, then manages them in a personal library.

## Brand & Tone
- **Aesthetic**: Dark academia - warm, moody, literary, scholarly
- **Feel**: Like an old library card catalog come to digital life, with soul and character
- **Voice**: Warm, inviting, slightly nostalgic but not dated

## Anti-References
- NOT a generic SaaS dashboard with blue/gray default palette
- NOT clinical or sterile
- NOT generic AI product with neon/cyber aesthetics
- NOT corporate or enterprise-feeling

## Design Principles
1. **Prioritize the book shelf/library view** - the collection is the heart of the app
2. **Make the scanner feel magical** - the AI detection should feel wondrous, not clinical
3. **Every pixel should feel intentional** - nothing generic, everything has a reason
4. **Warmth over efficiency** - a slower, more deliberate experience that feels human
5. **Books are the heroes** - imagery of books takes center stage

## Register
**Product** - This is an app UI where design serves the functional purpose of book management.
